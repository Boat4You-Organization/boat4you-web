FAQ: Sailing Yacht Charter from Vibo Marina

What does the marina berth fee include?

Berth fees encompass mooring position, water, electricity, and 24-hour security surveillance. Fuel purchases, provisioning, and repair services are charged separately based on usage. Boat4You provides transparent itemization prior to charter confirmation.

How early should I arrive for departure?

Plan arrival at Vibo Marina minimum four hours before scheduled departure. This timeframe accommodates check-in procedures, safety briefing, vessel inspection, and provisioning final confirmation without rushed transitions.

Can the marina arrange provisioning?

Yes, our provisioning coordination connects you with approved suppliers offering fresh produce, provisions, and specialty items. Pre-voyage shopping lists are welcome, allowing to arrange pre-stocking before your arrival.

What repair services are available?

Emergency mechanical support, electrical troubleshooting, and basic repairs can be arranged through our marina partnership network. Complex repairs may require shipyard facilities located approximately 30 kilometres away in commercial harbour areas.

Is professional crew available for extended voyages?

Absolutely. Experienced professional captains and crew can be arranged for multi-week expeditions, international passages, or vessels requiring specialized sailing expertise. Crew costs are itemized separately and billed daily or per-voyage.
