Weather, Navigation, and Operational Conditions

Sardinia's northeast coast enjoys Mediterranean conditions with warm, dry summers (June–September) and mild winters. Summer sea temperatures reach 24–28°C, ideal for swimming and snorkeling. The prevailing Maestrale wind (northwest, 8–18 knots) provides pleasant breezes; power catamarans operate unaffected by wind conditions, allowing flexible passage planning and relaxed anchorages.

Spring (April–May) and autumn (September–October) present optimal conditions: steady temperatures, comfortable water conditions (18–24°C), and fewer day-trippers crowding popular destinations. Winter months (November–March) are navigable but require attentiveness to occasional mistral storms and variable winds. Cannigione's protected basin and robust mooring systems provide secure harbor during rough weather.

Our team monitors Meteo Mediterranean forecasts, swell patterns, and tidal streams, enabling safe and enjoyable cruising. Mobile weather apps integrated with vessel navigation systems offer 10-day outlooks. Emergency communication systems and 24/7 support ensure assistance if conditions deteriorate unexpectedly.
