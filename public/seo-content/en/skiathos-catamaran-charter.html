Skiathos: Beaches, Culture & Adventure

Koukounaries Beach consistently ranks among Greece's finest—3km of golden sand backed by pine forest, perfect for swimming and water sports. Lalaria Beach, accessible only by sea, reveals limestone cliffs and crystalline turquoise waters ideal for catamaran visitors seeking seclusion.

The Bourtzi peninsula features a 15th-century Venetian fortress guarding Skiathos Harbour, now a cultural landmark and sunset viewpoint. Skiathos town offers tavernas, cafes, boutique shopping, and vibrant nightlife—all within walking distance of the Old Harbour where charter catamarans moor.

Skiathos to Skopelos & Alonissos: Perfect Day & Multi-Day Itineraries

Skopelos lies just 15 nautical miles east of Skiathos, celebrated for "Mamma Mia!" filming locations, traditional Chora architecture, and the hidden Loutraki beach anchorage. A catamaran voyage takes 2–3 hours, leaving full days for beach exploration and taverna lunches in authentic villages.

Alonissos, 20nm further east, protects the National Marine Park where Mediterranean monk seals inhabit sea caves. Catamaran shallow draft enables access to marine reserve anchorages forbidden to larger vessels. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical weather for catamaran sailing in Skiathos during summer?

June through September brings warm, sunny weather with afternoon Meltemi winds (15-25 kts from the north). These consistent winds provide exhilarating sailing yet rarely exceed manageable levels for crewed charters. Water temperatures reach 26–28°C, perfect for swimming and water sports.

Are catamarans suitable for families with young children?

Absolutely. Catamarans' stability, spacious decks, and shallow draft (enabling beach landings) suit families excellently. Modern catamarans offer private cabins, full toilets, and comfortable bunks. Shallow anchorages near beaches allow safe swimming. Crewed options ensure professional child supervision and family-friendly meal preparation.

How long does sailing from Skiathos to Skopelos take on a catamaran?

Under typical summer conditions with Meltemi winds, the 15nm passage takes 2–3 hours. Morning departures allow lunch at Skopelos Chora or early-afternoon arrival at Loutraki, maximizing daylight for anchoring and beach exploration.

Can I combine catamaran sailing with land activities in Skiathos?

Yes. Berth your catamaran overnight at Skiathos Old Harbour, then explore the town on foot. Restaurants, shops, museums, and cultural events are accessible during evening hours. Some charterers combine 5-day sailing with 2 days based at Skiathos accommodations for comprehensive island exploration.
