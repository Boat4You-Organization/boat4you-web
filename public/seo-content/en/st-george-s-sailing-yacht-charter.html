Monohull Performance in Caribbean Trade Winds

The northeast trade winds that blow consistently from December through May represent perfect conditions for monohull sailing yachts. These steady 15-25 knot breezes allow impressive sailing speeds while maintaining manageable weather conditions. A monohull's deeper draft and narrower beam respond eagerly to these winds, delivering thrilling sailing experiences that catamaran designs simply cannot match.
