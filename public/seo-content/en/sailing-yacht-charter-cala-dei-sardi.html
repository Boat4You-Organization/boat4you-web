Environmental Stewardship and Sustainable Sailing

Cala dei Sardi's environmental protection philosophy shapes sailing operations. Anchoring procedures utilize buoy systems preventing seagrass meadow damage. Electric auxiliary motors replace diesel engines within the bay—critical for preserving underwater ecosystems. Strict regulations limit day-anchoring density, protecting fragile Mediterranean marine habitats.'s commitment to sustainable sailing operations prioritizes environmental preservation alongside guest enjoyment.

Mediterranean seagrass meadows (Posidonia oceanica) create critical nurseries for fish species and provide oxygen production essential for coastal ecosystems. Protecting these meadows requires anchoring discipline—precise placement within designated buoy systems, careful weight distribution, and avoidance of dragging during weather.
