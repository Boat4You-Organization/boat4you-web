Why Charter a Catamaran from Lefkas Main Port?

Lefkas main port—the historic municipal harbour nestled along Lefkada town's waterfront—serves as the primary regional embarkation point for Boat4You catamaran charters across the Ionian Islands. Unlike dedicated marinas elsewhere, this central quayside location places you steps from the archaeological museum, harbour-side tavernas, and the vibrant Lefkada waterfront.

The main port's strategic position makes it ideal for sailors planning multi-day passages to Meganisi, Ithaca, Kefalonia's Fiskardo, or the quieter reaches of Kastos and Kalamos. Modern med-mooring berthing accommodates both bareboat and crewed catamaran charters with ease.

Seasonal Sailing Patterns and Wind Predictability

The Ionian Islands' reputation for consistent, moderate summer winds stems from the Maistro's northwesterly flow. Lefkas main port benefits from this pattern: morning calm water ideal for engine-assisted exits, then reliable 12–15-knot winds by midday supporting comfortable sailing angles toward any regional destination.

Autumn months (September–October) bring occasional strong Sirocco blasts from the southeast but also reward patient skippers with reduced crowding and clearer anchorages. Winter charters suit only expert crews; spring (April–May) offers rebuilding conditions and blooming wildflower landscapes.
