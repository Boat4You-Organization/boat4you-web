Poseidon Marina Infrastructure and Services

Poseidon Marina's 400 berths accommodate everything from small sailboats to large motor yachts, with water depth and fixed infrastructure suitable for vessels up to 60 feet. Concrete piers feature modern mooring cleats, power pedestals with 230V/380V connections, and fresh water supply points. Your vessel is positioned to ensure optimal space and accessibility during your charter.

A fully-staffed fuel dock operates daily, providing diesel and gasoline in quantities suitable for pre-charter tank-filling or mid-voyage top-ups. The marina's repair and maintenance facility includes haul-out capacity, professional mechanics, and parts suppliers for emergency repairs. While most charter vessels are already fully serviced and ready for departure, this on-site capability provides security for unexpected situations during your voyage.

Shore-power pedestals throughout the marina provide European-standard 230V electrical connections, allowing battery charging and refrigeration operation during multi-day mooring periods. Fresh water connections feature quality Mediterranean-source water through closed-system supply. A modern pump-out facility maintains sanitary conditions and environmental standards across the entire marina basin.

Sailing Conditions from Poseidon Marina

Departures from Poseidon Marina place you within hours of extraordinary sailing destinations. Vulcano is approximately 4–5 hours of downwind sailing; Lipari, 5–6 hours; and Stromboli, 8–10 hours of fast passages. The open-water route from Milazzo to the Aeolian chain benefits from consistent northwesterly winds during May–September, offering favorable conditions for diverse sailing experiences.

Coastal sailing along Sicily's northern shore—departing from Poseidon Marina eastward toward Messina—offers protected waters, numerous anchorages, and charming villages suitable for day-sailing and exploring. These routes are ideal for crews preferring gentler conditions or shorter passages while still immersing themselves in Mediterranean sailing culture.

Your captain's or crew's local knowledge ensures optimal weather timing. Departures are planned to avoid forecasted squalls, leverage favorable wind shifts, and arrive at your next anchorage during daylight hours. The Poseidon Marina harbormaster's daily weather briefings inform strategic departure decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time can I arrive for check-in at Poseidon Marina?

Standard check-in begins at 4:00 PM on your charter start date. Arriving earlier in the day allows time to explore Milazzo, purchase provisions, or relax before boarding. Your team can arrange early reception at the marina if you arrive earlier, though your vessel may not be ready for immediate departure until late afternoon.

Can I pre-purchase provisions and have them delivered to my yacht at Poseidon Marina?

Yes, absolutely. Provide with your dietary preferences, provisioning lists, and budget at booking. Our team purchases items from local suppliers and has them delivered to your vessel prior to your arrival. This option is particularly valuable for guests arriving tired from travel or those unfamiliar with Italian markets and product availability.

Does Poseidon Marina provide Internet and mobile phone connectivity?

Poseidon Marina features wireless internet in reception and restaurant areas. Mobile phone coverage throughout Milazzo and the Aeolian Islands is reliable from European carriers (roaming for international visitors). Once at sea, your yacht maintains VHF communication with mainland-based support. Satellite phone systems can be added as optional equipment for continuous connectivity during your voyage.

What is the process for checking out and returning my yacht?

Return to Poseidon Marina on your charter's final day, typically by mid-afternoon to allow checkout procedures. Marina staff conduct a brief vessel inspection for damage or maintenance needs. Your coordinator reviews your charter log and completes final documentation. The vessel is then transferred to marina staff for cleaning and preparation for the next charter.

Are there opportunities to extend my charter or adjust itineraries during the voyage?

Yes, If weather or circumstances suggest extending your charter, your captain can coordinate extended berthing at your current location, extending your original checkout date at appropriate daily rates. Itinerary adjustments based on weather, group preferences, or opportunities for specific experiences can be implemented at your captain's discretion, provided they align with checkout timing and your broader charter goals.
