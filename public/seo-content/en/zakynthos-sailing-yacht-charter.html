Zakynthos: Ionian Island Paradise

Zakynthos (Zante) is the southernmost major Ionian island, famous for its dramatic landscape, crystal waters, and unique marine biodiversity. Navagio Beach features a shipwreck surrounded by towering cliffs—the Ionian's most iconic image. The Blue Caves offer otherworldly swimming and snorkeling. Loggerhead sea turtles nest on protected beaches. This natural grandeur attracts sailors, swimmers, and nature enthusiasts worldwide.
