Sailing Conditions from Ligia

The summer Maistro wind delivers 10–18 knots of northwest breeze, ideal for the 2–4 hour reaches to neighboring islands. From Ligia, you're positioned between the shelter of Lefkada's lee and the open Ionian—a goldilocks location. The protected harbor allows morning departures without rush; light winds or calms mean short engine runs to your first anchorage.

Lygia Marina Facilities

The municipal marina has 80 berths, professional harbor watch, and consistent fresh water and electricity. Showers and toilets are clean. The harbor's protected nature—ringed by low hills and close-set breakwaters—makes mooring secure in southerly swells that can affect more exposed anchorages. Winter sailing is possible; the harbor's shelter is exceptional.

Sailing Routes from Ligia

North 30 minutes to Nikiana and its quiet taverna bay. Continue north to Desimi and Episkopi for pristine solitude. South, Nidri sits 40 minutes downwind; from there, the Meganisi strait opens toward Skorpios and the mainland coast. West into the open Ionian takes you to Kalamos (3–4 hours) and eventually Kastos. Every route is manageable for confident sailors, and the Maistro's consistency makes planning easy.
