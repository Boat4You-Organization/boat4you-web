Sailing Routes: Ionian Islands from Preveza Marina

Summer Maistro winds (12–18 knots from the north) create ideal sailing conditions across the Ionian. From Preveza Marina, direct courses lead to Lefkada (5 nm south), the region's flagship sailing destination. South-facing beaches, protected anchorages near Nydri, and vibrant beach club culture make Lefkada essential for any Ionian itinerary.

Nearby anchorages include Meganisi (Spilia with its dramatic sea caves), Kalamos (unspoiled and quiet), and Kastos (authentic island charm). Ambitious crews push south to Ithaca and Kefalonia, or venture northwest to Paxos and its emerald sea grottos.

Summer Sailing Season in the Ionian

May through September delivers predictable Maistro conditions. June–August peak season combines warm, sunny weather with steady winds ideal for training and relaxation. May and September offer equally reliable sailing with fewer tourists and quieter anchorages, perfect for those seeking solitude.

Daily wind patterns typically see morning calm—ideal for swimming and snorkeling—with afternoon breeze arriving around midday, allowing evening returns to sheltered anchorages. This rhythm suits both ambitious sailing and leisurely cruising.
