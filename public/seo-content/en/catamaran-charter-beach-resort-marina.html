Routes from Beach Resort Marina

The Abaco Coastal Cruise (7–10 days) is the signature itinerary. Depart Marsh Harbour heading south to Hope Town (Elbow Cay), explore the famous candy-striped lighthouse and Victorian architecture, then return north to Green Turtle Cay and Guana Cay. This loop covers the Sea of Abaco's most accessible destinations, offering cultural immersion and pristine anchorages without confronting open ocean.

Northern Abaco Exploration (7–10 days) heads north from Marsh Harbour to Treasure Cay, Walker's Cay (northern limit of protection), and remote cays offering genuine isolation while remaining within protected waters. This route suits experienced crews comfortable navigating reef passages and seeking remote anchorages.

Extended Abaco + Berry Islands (10–12 days) combines the Abaco coastal cruise with a northeastern crossing to the Berry Islands' remote anchorages — Cloud Cay, Stranger's Cay, and Chub Cay. This route involves longer passages (30–40 nm typical) and greater self-reliance but rewards with genuine wilderness sailing and virtually zero other vessels at anchor.
