Frequently Asked Questions

What sailing qualifications are required?

Bareboat charters require ICC or equivalent national qualifications. Skippered and crewed charters have no requirements—your professional sailing crew handles navigation and seamanship. accommodates all experience levels.

How do monohull sailing yachts compare to catamarans?

Monohulls offer traditional sailing dynamics, engaging helm response, and deeper seamanship involvement. Catamarans provide comfort and stability with reduced sailing engagement. Choose monohulls for active sailing; catamarans for relaxation.

Can my family learn sailing on a Piso Livadi yacht?

Yes. Skippered charters with professional instruction suit families. Children engage at their level; adults develop skills progressively. Shared sailing challenges create lasting family bonding and achievement satisfaction.

What weather should I expect?

Consistent meltemia winds (15–25 knots in summer) provide reliable sailing conditions. Morning breezes build throughout the day. Evening moderating allows peaceful anchorage arrival. Weather patterns are exceptionally predictable May-October.
