Zea Marina: Where Ancient Piraeus Meets Modern Sailing

Zea Marina occupies one of Piraeus' three historic harbors, originally built during the Classical period as a naval shipyard for Athenian triremes. Today, Neoclassical mansions line the quays, archaeological sites dot the peninsula, and the statue of Themistocles watches over the water. Boat4You's Zea Marina catamarans blend modern marine engineering with this profound historical context—sailing toward the Saronic Islands from Athens' oldest sailing harbor, connecting present-day cruising with millennia of Mediterranean maritime tradition.
