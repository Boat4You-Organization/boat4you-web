Abacos Sailing from TradeWinds' Premium Platform

The Abacos' protected waters and intimate sailing character are perfectly suited to TradeWinds' premium catamaran platform. The Sea of Abaco offers 7–10 days of manageable, beautiful sailing — you're rarely confronting serious open-ocean conditions, allowing you to focus on the sailing itself, island exploration, and group social experience rather than navigation challenge.

The Classic Hope Town Itinerary (7 days) departs Marsh Harbour, sails south to Hope Town (Elbow Cay), spends 2–3 days exploring the famous candy-striped lighthouse and Victorian architecture, then returns north to Green Turtle Cay for cultural immersion before returning to Marsh Harbour. This route balances island culture with pristine anchorages and serves as a perfect introduction to the Abacos for first-time sailors or experienced crews wanting relaxed sailing.

Extended Abaco Northern Exploration (10 days) heads north from Marsh Harbour to Treasure Cay, Walker's Cay, and remote northern cays, offering greater sailing challenges and genuine wilderness anchorages while remaining within the protected Sea of Abaco. This route suits experienced crews comfortable with reef navigation and seeking remoteness.

Customized Multi-Day Expedition (7–14 days) combines your specific interests — extended anchoring at particular islands, specialized exploration (snorkeling, photography, cultural immersion), or family-focused destinations. TradeWinds' personalized service model means your itinerary is truly customized rather than templated.
