Kornati Archipelago Access & Island Routes

The Kornati National Park lies approximately twelve nautical miles northeast of Tribunj. A catamaran departs at dawn and reaches the southern islands (Kornat, Žut) by mid-morning, allowing full-day exploration. The limestone plateaus, dramatic cliffs, and translucent anchorages exemplify Mediterranean grandeur. Best Seasons for Catamaran Charters

Spring (April–May) brings warming air (18–22°C), calm mornings, and refreshing conditions ideal for comfortable sailing. Summer (June–August) offers reliable wind, warm water (23–25°C), and vibrant island activity—though mid-July crowding is pronounced. Autumn (September–October) combines warm swimming conditions (22–23°C), consistent breezes, and manageable crowds. Winter sailing is possible but involves shorter days and occasional rough passages; most commercial charters operate April through October.
