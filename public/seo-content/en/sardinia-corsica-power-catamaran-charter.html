Frequently Asked Questions

What boating experience is required to bareboat charter a power catamaran?

Power catamaran bareboat charters require basic boating competency and familiarity with motor vessel operation. assesses your boating background and provides comprehensive orientation. Non-experienced mariners should consider skippered charters where professional crews handle navigation and docking.

When is the best season for power catamaran cruising in Sardinia and Corsica?

Summer months (June-September) offer warm weather and calm conditions, though crowds peak in August. Spring (April-May) and autumn (September-October) provide excellent conditions with fewer tourists. Winter cruising is possible but involves unpredictable weather patterns and reduced service availability at shore facilities.

Can power catamarans anchor in shallow water and remote locations?

Power catamarans feature shallow draft capability (typically 1.2-1.5 meters) allowing anchoring in many protected bays inaccessible to monohull vessels. Many pristine Maddalena Archipelago anchorages welcome power catamaran cruising.

Are water sports equipment and activities included in power catamaran charters?

Each power catamaran includes snorkeling equipment, paddleboards, and dinghy tenders. We can arrange SCUBA certifications, jet ski rentals, fishing excursions, or waterski instruction. Anchorages throughout the region provide excellent opportunities for swimming, snorkeling, and water-based family activities.
