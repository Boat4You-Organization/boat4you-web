Are motorboats suitable for families with children?

Excellent. Motorboats offer stability, easy deck access, and safe swimming platforms. Fast transit between islands means less time at sea and more time exploring beaches. Calm anchorages in Brioni, Cres, and Lošinj are ideal for families.
