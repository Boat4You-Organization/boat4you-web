Operating Seasons & Conditions

Spring (April–May) features calm seas, predictable weather, and water temperatures rising to 18–19°C. Ideal for inexperienced crews and guests prioritizing comfort over wind challenges. Summer (June–August) offers reliable cruising conditions, though July–August crowds and heat intensify. Fuel consumption rises with higher ambient temperature and air-conditioning demand. Autumn (September–October) combines warm water (23–24°C), moderate conditions, and reduced fuel costs—often preferred by experienced crews prioritizing operational economy.
