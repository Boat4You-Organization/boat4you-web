Top Motorboat Destinations from Veruda

Brijuni National Park, 8 nautical miles north-west, represents the quintessential motorboat day trip. A 30-minute journey under cruising power delivers you to 14 protected islands offering archaeology sites, pine-fringed anchorages, and exceptional snorkelling. Motorboats' responsive handling permits exploring island coves, anchoring near Byzantine ruins, and navigating narrow passages inaccessible to larger vessels.

Fažana village, 5 kilometres north, combines working-harbour authenticity with motorboat hospitality. The village serves as a secondary fuel and provisioning hub; waterfront restaurants cater specifically to day-trippers. A motorboat circuit to Fažana, morning exploration of nearby Brijuni, and lunchtime return enables efficient full-day itineraries with minimal time investment.

Kamenjak peninsula—Istria's southernmost point—showcases dramatic limestone cliffs, turquoise waters, and protected coves. The 20-nautical-mile passage (approximately 45 minutes motorboat cruising) accesses nature reserve anchorages, pristine beaches, and secluded swimming areas. Motorboats excel here: quick transit, flexible timing, and easy repositioning between multiple anchorages within the reserve.

Rovinj, Istria's most picturesque waterfront town, lies 35 nautical miles north-west (approximately 90 minutes by motorboat). The journey rewards explorers: coastal views, island passages, and arrival at Adriatic's most visually compelling harbour. Multi-day motorboat charters from Veruda often culminate with overnight mooring at Rovinj, enabling waterfront dining and medieval town exploration.
