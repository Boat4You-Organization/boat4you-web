Catamaran Charter from Marina Novi

Marina Novi is the operational cornerstone of catamaran sailing in the Kvarner Gulf. Located in Novi Vinodolski—a town steeped in medieval maritime heritage—this dedicated marina provides secure mooring, fuel dock, water, professional reception, and comprehensive support for Boat4You's 3 catamarans. Unlike anchoring in open water, Marina Novi's structured facilities, breakwater protection, and professional staff ensure your catamaran charter is seamlessly coordinated from embarkation through return.

Marina Novi: Purpose-Built Catamaran Base

Marina Novi is specifically designed for catamaran operations and visiting charter fleets. The facility features dedicated berths accommodating multi-hulled vessels up to 50 feet, full-time reception, fuel dock (7 AM–6 PM daily), potable water at each berth, shore-powered electricity, Wi-Fi throughout the marina, and a well-stocked chandlery. The concrete breakwater provides reliable protection from summer Maestral winds and provides a secure operational base for your catamaran exploration of the Kvarner.

Boat4You's three catamarans are strategically berthed at Marina Novi, allowing quick provisioning, fuel coordination, and departure without delay. The marina's professional staff manage mooring logistics, assist with passage planning, and provide real-time weather updates and local sailing knowledge essential for Kvarner navigation.

Berth Allocation & Mooring at Marina Novi

Catamarans receive dedicated berths at Marina Novi, with side-tied configurations allowing rapid departure. Mooring fees range €2.00–€2.80 per meter per night, depending on vessel length. Boat4You pre-coordinates your berth assignment; reception staff assist with docking procedures, especially for first-time visitors. Most catamaran crews moor overnight initially for provisioning and preparation, then undertake island-hopping expeditions, returning to secure mooring on final charter nights.

Extended mooring at Marina Novi is accommodated. If your catamaran charter exceeds one week, Marina Novi coordinates reduced nightly rates and facilitates re-provisioning and rest days between sailing expeditions.

Fuel Dock & Refueling Operations

Marina Novi's fuel dock operates daily 7 AM–6 PM, supplying diesel and petrol. Boat4You's catamarans (typically 200–300 liter fuel tanks) refill within 15–25 minutes. The dock accepts cash and card payments; Boat4You arranges direct billing to your charter account, eliminating onsite financial hassles. Fuel pricing reflects current market rates; marina staff provide consumption estimates based on your planned itinerary and cruising speeds.

Most catamaran crews refuel immediately upon arrival at Marina Novi, ensuring full tanks before island expeditions. The fuel dock's convenient location streamlines this critical logistical step.

Water Dock & Freshwater Provisioning

Dedicated water dock adjacent to fuel provides pressurized freshwater tank fills at €0.50–€1.00 per 100 liters. Most catamarans carry 200–300 liter freshwater tanks; full refills take 15–20 minutes. Water dock availability (7 AM–6 PM) means convenient top-ups before day trips or during evening returns.

Chandlery, Spares & Technical Support

Marina Novi's chandlery stocks essential catamaran spares: engine oil, filters, rope, shackles, safety equipment, and navigation charts. If maintenance needs arise, marina staff can arrange technician assistance or provide temporary repair guidance. maintains 24/7 telephone support for emergencies or unexpected mechanical issues.

Common provisioning items—ice, sunscreen, first-aid supplies, marine-grade cleaning products—are immediately available. Chandlery staff are knowledgeable about Kvarner sailing conditions and recommend routes, anchorages, and seasonal navigation considerations relevant to catamaran operations.

Laundry, Showers & Hygiene Facilities

Modern ablution blocks feature hot-water showers, laundry machines, and clean restrooms. After days sailing and anchoring, these amenities provide essential comfort. Laundry operates on a coin-feed system; detergent is available at the marina office. Many catamaran crews dedicate one morning to laundry, provisioning, and supplies restocking while the vessel rests at its berth.

Parking & Arrival Logistics

Marina Novi offers secure parking for rental vehicles throughout your catamaran charter. Most crews park upon arrival and retrieve their vehicles only at departure. Secure parking at the marina gates ensures vehicle safety; parking fees are included in 's overall package. This simplifies arrival and departure logistics considerably.

Getting to Marina Novi: Access & Transfers

Novi Vinodolski is 30 kilometers south of Rijeka via direct highway (30-minute drive). Rijeka International Airport (Rijeka Krk Airport) serves arrivals 50 kilometers away. Many catamaran crews arrange car rentals in Rijeka and drive directly to Marina Novi for check-in. Alternatively, ferry services from Rijeka and nearby towns provide public transit options.

Marina Novi sits within walking distance of Novi Vinodolski town center (5–10 minutes). Restaurants, supermarkets, wine shops, and fresh provisioning are easily accessible on foot, combining marina convenience with urban amenities.

Catamaran Routes from Marina Novi

Krk Island lies 8 nautical miles south—a straightforward 2-3 hour catamaran sail, ideal for confidence-building first-day passages. Rab Island (18 nautical miles southwest) is reachable by mid-afternoon, offering medieval towns, excellent harbors, and pristine northern anchorages. Pag Island (25 nautical miles southeast) provides dramatic karst landscapes and multiple harbor options.

Lesser-known islets (Goli Otok, Prvić, Grgur) offer exceptional snorkeling, minimal crowds, and pristine anchorages. Marina staff offer real-time advice on wind conditions, current anchorage quality, and optimal catamaran routing.

Provisioning & Kvarner Culinary Tradition

Novi Vinodolski's waterfront market and supermarkets stock fresh vegetables, bread, local cheese, and daily fish catches. The town specializes in Kvarner culinary traditions: seafood stews, pasta with scampi, Teran red wine, and locally pressed olive oil. Most catamaran crews provision entirely before departure; markets are convenient and prices moderate compared to major resort towns.

Secondary provisioning at island harbors (Krk, Rab, Novalja) replenishes supplies mid-charter. Local restaurants throughout the region serve traditional Kvarner cuisine at reasonable prices; many establishments offer group discounts and welcome sailor crews.

Seasonal Sailing Patterns & Weather

Marina Novi's breakwater provides excellent protection from summer Maestral winds. Spring and autumn offer moderate wind conditions optimal for catamaran sailing. Summer (June–August) brings reliable weather but also increased boat traffic; shoulder seasons provide quieter marina operations and more peaceful anchorages. operates catamaran charters May–October, with June and September–October consistently rated best seasons.

FAQ: Catamaran Charters from Marina Novi

What are Marina Novi's mooring costs?

Nightly mooring fees are typically €2.00–€2.80 per meter of boat length. A 35-foot catamaran costs approximately €70–€98 nightly. Weekly rates sometimes offer modest discounts. Search Boat4You for transparent pricing for your specific vessel and dates.

What's included in Marina Novi's mooring fee?

Mooring fees cover berth assignment, power, water access, Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, shower blocks, and facility maintenance. Fuel, water consumption, provisions, and restaurant meals are additional.

Can I provision my catamaran before departing Marina Novi?

Absolutely. Waterfront markets, supermarkets, and chandlery are within walking distance. Most catamaran crews spend their first morning provisioning, stocking galley and water tanks, then depart early afternoon once supplies are secured.

How long does it take to sail from Marina Novi to Rab Island?

Typical sailing time is 4–5 hours under favorable conditions. Catamaran passages are often faster than monohull alternatives due to higher cruising speeds. Wind direction, current, and desired route variations influence actual timing.

What facilities are available if I need emergency repairs at Marina Novi?

Marina staff can arrange professional technicians for most mechanical emergencies.

Is Wi-Fi reliable at Marina Novi?

Yes, Marina Novi provides robust Wi-Fi coverage throughout the facility. Many catamaran crews use connectivity for uploading photos, communicating with family, and receiving weather updates. also offers satellite communication if you venture to remote anchorages beyond cellular range.
