Seasonal Considerations and Weather Reliability

Summer months (June-September) deliver warm, sunny weather ideal for Mediterranean cruising. The meltemi—predictable northwesterly wind—rarely disrupts motor yacht operations, though morning departures capitalise on calmer conditions and afternoon swimming stops time before stronger winds develop. Water temperatures reach comfortable 28°C, enabling extended swimming without wetsuits.

Spring (April-May) and autumn (September-October) offer ideal conditions: warm water, reliable weather, reduced crowds, and accessible pricing. These shoulder seasons attract experienced travellers preferring moderate temperatures, fewer vessels, and more authentic local experiences.

Winter sailing (November-March) suits flexible passengers valuing solitude and exceptional pricing. Weather remains less predictable, requiring itinerary flexibility, but motor yachts' reliability ensures steady progress regardless of wind conditions. Air conditioning ensures comfortable accommodation despite cooler temperatures.
