Top Motorboat Destinations from Polesana

Motorboats excel here—quick transit enables flexible timing, easy repositioning between multiple anchorages, and reliable power for return journeys if unexpected weather emerges.

Rovinj, Istria's most picturesque waterfront town, lies 90 minutes north-west by motorboat. Multi-day motorboat charters culminate with Rovinj overnight mooring, enabling waterfront dining and medieval town exploration. The journey delivers panoramic coastal views; arrival at Rovinj's distinctive harbour—terracotta rooftops cascading toward turquoise waters—rewards the passage.
