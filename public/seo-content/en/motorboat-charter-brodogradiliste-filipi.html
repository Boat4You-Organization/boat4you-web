Seasons, Weather & Best Times to Visit

Spring (April–May) combines warm days (18–22°C), calm mornings perfect for open-motorboat exploration, and manageable crowds. Water temperatures average 17°C. Summer (June–August) brings sunshine, warmer water (21–24°C), and reliable afternoon Maestral winds. Mid-July to mid-August sees peak boat traffic; early June and late August offer compromise.

Autumn (September–October) is ideal: stable weather, warm water (20–22°C), fewer boats, and exceptional underwater visibility. Motorboats—unwind-dependent—operate safely even in light rain. Winter (November–March) is possible for experienced crews but closes most commercial operations.
