FAQs About Catamaran Charter at Preveza Marina

What's included in a catamaran charter package?

Packages typically include the vessel, onboard equipment (sails, navigation, safety gear), linens, and end-of-week cleaning. Fuel, provisioning, and optional skippers or crew are booked separately. Boat4You clarifies all inclusions during booking.

Can bareboat renters handle a catamaran at Preveza Marina?

Yes, if you hold RYA or equivalent sailing credentials. Catamarans are intuitive once crew experience multihull dynamics. Boat4You offers pre-departure briefings and 24/7 support. First-time multihull sailors may prefer a professional skipper.

What's the best time to charter at Preveza Marina?

June through August offer peak conditions—warm weather, steady Maistro winds, and bustling island life. May and September provide quieter sailing with equally reliable breeze. Winter is off-season; most charter fleets shift to haul-out facilities like Cleopatra Marina.

How far south can we sail from Preveza Marina?

Multi-day passages reach Kefalonia and Ithaca (20+ nm), or venture to Zakynthos further south. Shorter hops to Lefkada and Meganisi suit week-long charters. customizes itineraries based on crew experience and available time.

Are provisioning services available at Preveza Marina?

The marina's modern chandlery stocks provisions, ice, and equipment. Local markets and supermarkets near the marina provide fresh produce and groceries. Many crews pre-arrange catering or book provisioning services through our partners.
