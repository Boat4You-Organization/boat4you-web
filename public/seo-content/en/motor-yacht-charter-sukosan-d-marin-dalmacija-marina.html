Motor Yacht Routes: Elite Anchorages & Hidden Coves

Kornati National Park, 20 nautical miles south (3 hours), offers exclusive island anchorages and protected deep-water mooring. Motor yachts position in prestigious spots like Sipan's sheltered bay and Katić's exclusive fish-restaurant cove. The limestone landscape and underwater clarity create dramatic backdrops for entertaining and photography.

Telašćica Bay, 25 nautical miles southwest (4 hours), provides one of the Mediterranean's most prestigious anchorages. Sheer 150-meter limestone cliffs, protected deep-water mooring, and the unique saltwater lake (Mir) create an unforgettable setting. Motor yachts anchor while crews explore by tender and enjoy contemplative retreats in absolute privacy.

Pašman and Ugljan islands (8–12 nautical miles, 1.5–2 hours) offer exclusive village anchorages and upscale waterfront restaurants welcoming motor yacht clientele. Multi-day charters anchor between these islands, exploring local culture and enjoying intimate dinners at harborside establishments.

Dugi Otok's southern coast (15–20 nautical miles, 2–3 hours) features pristine Sakarun beach and remote anchorages—destinations exclusive to private yachts. Motor yachts anchor in shallow, sheltered waters while crews enjoy beach tenders, swimming, and sunset aperitifs.
