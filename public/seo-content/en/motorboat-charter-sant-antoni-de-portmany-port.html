Port Infrastructure for Motorboat Operations

Sant Antoni port has developed comprehensive facilities specifically designed for motorboat operations. Deep-water berths accommodate vessels from 20 to 60+ feet, with modern mooring buoys, shore power connections (220V/380V), freshwater connections, and professional fuel services. The harbor's natural characteristics—wide opening, protected anchorage, sandy holding—provide excellent operational conditions for motorboats of all sizes.

Professional reception facilities operate 24/7, offering customs clearance support, maritime documentation assistance, and multilingual staff familiar with international motorboat operations. Waste management infrastructure meets EU standards, and fuel quality adheres to ISO specifications ensuring reliable engine performance for extended passages.

Day Hire Operations from Sant Antoni Port

Standard day-hire motorboat charters typically run 9 AM to 5 PM or 2 PM to 10 PM, with flexible extensions available. The morning departure allows breakfast provisions, 30-minute briefing covering vessel systems and local maritime protocols, and 6-7 hours of active cruising. Most day-hire guests visit 2-3 destinations—Cala Salada (30 min from port), Cala Bassa (35 min), and a lunch stop at beach club establishments.

The Formentera day trip exemplifies motorboat flexibility: depart 9 AM, reach Ses Illetes beach within 50 minutes, spend 5 hours swimming and beach clubbing, return by 6 PM. This itinerary is impossible under sail (3+ hours each direction) but comfortably achievable with motorboat transportation. Many guests describe motorboat day hires as "island-hopping"—the convenience of motorized travel opening otherwise inaccessible itineraries.

Provisioning and Port Services from Sant Antoni

FAQ

What happens if weather deteriorates during our motorboat charter?

Sant Antoni port and nearby protected coves provide refuge within 15-30 minutes of any location. The Mediterranean's moderate conditions mean most weather deterioration occurs as brief thunderstorms rather than sustained rough conditions. maintains real-time monitoring and advises customers on safe alternatives when conditions shift unexpectedly.

Can we hire a professional captain for motorboat day hire?

Absolutely. Captained day charters add approximately EUR 150-250 depending on vessel size and itinerary complexity. Professional captains handle navigation, mooring, and safety while guests focus on enjoyment. This option suits mixed-experience groups, families with young children, or those prioritizing relaxation over boat operation.

What's included in motorboat charter pricing?

motorboat pricing includes vessel rental, safety equipment, navigation systems, basic provisioning supplies, and maritime insurance. Additional costs include fuel consumption, mooring fees (if returning to Sant Antoni port mid-charter), captain services, and optional water sports equipment rental. Can we visit multiple islands in a single day via motorboat?

Yes. A typical day covers Sant Antoni (departure) → Cala Salada (30 min) → Cala Bassa (35 min) → lunch destination (45 min) → return (30 min)—four locations in one day with multiple hours at each. Extended day-hire (8-10 hours) permits reaching Formentera's Ses Illetes (50 min), spending 4-5 hours, and returning by evening—a complete Formentera day trip impossible under sail alone.

Is Sant Antoni port convenient for early morning or late-evening departures?

Yes. Professional port staff operate 24/7, permitting flexibility in check-out timing. Early morning departures (7-8 AM) allow additional cruising hours. Late-evening returns (10-11 PM) extend adventure time during summer months with extended daylight. Pre-arrangement ensures crew availability and vessel readiness for non-standard timing.
