Seasons, Climate, and Conditions

The Saronic Gulf experiences Mediterranean climate: warm, dry summers (May-October); mild winters (November-April). Peak season (June-August) offers warm weather but larger visitor numbers. May and September provide ideal balance—reliable conditions, warm water, manageable crowds.

Water temperatures: 16°C (May) rising to 26°C (August), remaining comfortable through October. Summer temperatures exceed 30°C; spring/autumn offer moderate conditions (20–25°C). The Etesian winds (northerly, 10–15 knots) provide moderate conditions ideal for motor yacht operation.

Winter sailing is possible but less common; weather becomes variable and some facilities reduce hours. Boat4You recommends May-October for optimal conditions and full service availability.
