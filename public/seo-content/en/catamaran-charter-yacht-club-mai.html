Wind, Weather, and Seasonal Sailing Patterns

From Yacht Club Mai, seasonal wind patterns shape sailing experiences. Summer (July-August) brings consistent meltemi averaging 12-18 knots, creating predictable daily sailing rhythms: calm departures, spirited midday sailing, peaceful evening arrivals. June and September offer milder conditions—meltemi averaging 10-16 knots—with reduced tourist presence and exceptional sailing conditions.

Spring and autumn appeal to experienced sailors seeking variable wind patterns and technical challenges. Winter sailing (November-March) demands experience; stronger northerly winds and occasional gales present serious challenges for all but professional crews. However, winter's isolation, dramatic weather, and powerful sailing experiences attract adventure-seeking sailors. Yacht Club Mai's intimate scale ensures seasonal sailing reflects real conditions—not commercially-massaged weather promises.
