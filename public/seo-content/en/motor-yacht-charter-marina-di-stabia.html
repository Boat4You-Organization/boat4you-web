Destination Accessibility & Itinerary Flexibility

The town's dramatic clifftop setting, fragrant lemon terraces, and cosmopolitan restaurants await guest exploration while your vessel rests securely at Marina Grande.

The Amalfi Coast, stretching 25 nautical miles west from Marina di Stabia, represents Europe's most visually dramatic coastline.
