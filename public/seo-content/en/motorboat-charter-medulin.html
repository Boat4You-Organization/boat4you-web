Getting to Marina Medulin: Access & Transfers

Medulin is 10 kilometers south of Pula via direct road (15-minute drive). Pula International Airport (20 kilometers north) serves arrivals with taxi and rental-car options. Many motorboat crews arrange car rentals in Pula and drive directly to Marina Medulin for check-in.

Marina Medulin's location is 5–10 minutes walking distance from Medulin town center. Restaurants, supermarkets, and fresh-market provisioning are accessible on foot. This proximity balances marina convenience with easy access to urban amenities.

Motorboat Routes from Marina Medulin

The Kamenjak Nature Park headland lies 5 nautical miles south—a 20-minute motorboat run ideal for first-day confidence-building and snorkeling. Lošinj Island (20 nautical miles) is reachable by mid-morning, offering restaurants, fuel, and cultural attractions. Cres Island (25 nautical miles) extends northward with quieter anchorages. All these destinations are easily reached from Marina Medulin, with reliable return-journey fuel reserves.

Marina staff offer real-time advice on sea conditions, anchorage conditions, and optimal cruising speeds for fuel efficiency.

Provisioning & Local Food Culture

Medulin's market offers daily fresh vegetables, bread, local cheese, and seafood. Supermarkets stock international provisions and local wines—particularly Istrian Malvasia and Teran. Fish markets near the harbor supply daily catches perfect for grilling aboard your motorboat galley.

Most motorboat crews provision once or twice during a week-long rental, purchasing sufficient supplies to minimize shopping stops and maximize exploration time. Marina Medulin's proximity to shops makes provisioning straightforward.

Seasonal Operating Conditions & Weather

Marina Medulin's breakwater provides excellent shelter from summer Maestral winds. Spring and autumn offer moderate wind and calm conditions optimal for motorboat operation. Summer (June–August) brings reliable weather but also increased boat traffic; shoulder seasons offer quieter marina conditions and more peaceful anchorages.

The marina maintains current forecasts and wave-height updates. Motorboat operation is possible year-round, though peak charter season is May–October.
