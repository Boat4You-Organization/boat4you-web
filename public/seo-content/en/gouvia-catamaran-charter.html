Sailing Routes from Gouvia

From Gouvia, you can explore multiple sailing destinations. Head north along the Corfu coast to reach Kassiopi or Sidari, known for their dramatic cliffs and hidden beaches. Perfect Conditions for Summer Sailing

The Ionian summer wind pattern—the Maistro—blows consistently from the northwest, delivering 10–18 knots of steady breeze. Afternoons often see thermal-driven winds building gently. These conditions are ideal for catamaran sailing: stable, predictable, and exhilarating without being demanding. Even beginners with a skipper will feel the difference between a catamaran's forgiving motion and other vessel types.

Seasonal Wind & Weather Overview

The Ionian climate favours summer sailing from late May through September. The consistent Maistro wind (10–18 knots from the northwest) creates ideal conditions: strong enough for exhilarating sailing yet manageable and predictable. Daily thermal effects often strengthen the afternoon breeze. Sea temperatures range from 24°C in May to 26°C in August, perfect for swimming and snorkelling.

Spring (April–May) and autumn (September–October) offer gentler winds, fewer tourists, and equally rewarding experiences. Winter sailing is possible but less common due to variable Atlantic weather systems.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For peak season (July and August), reservations 4–6 months ahead secure the best fleet selection and pricing. Shoulder months (May, June, September) typically allow booking 6–8 weeks out.

What does the charter price include?

Base pricing usually covers the vessel, insurance, and marina fees at the home base. Fuel, provisioning, skipper service, end-cleaning, and tourist tax are generally added separately. Check the specific listing on Boat4You for a full breakdown.

Do I need a sailing licence?

For bareboat charter, a valid skipper licence plus a VHF certificate are typically required, and rules vary by country. If you don't hold one, hiring a professional skipper is straightforward — pricing runs roughly 150–220 euro per day plus meals.
