Seasonal Conditions & Weather Impacts

Spring (April–May) and autumn (September–October) offer ideal motorboat conditions: water temperatures of 16–21°C, moderate 10-15 knot winds (often crosswinds for RIBs), and manageable sea states. Summer (June–August) brings mirror-calm mornings and 15-20 knot afternoon sea-breezes, perfect for rapid island transits. Winter Bura (November–March) develops 30-40 knot northeast gusts and steep wave formations unsuitable for open RIB operation; however, protected bay transit and cabin motorboat cruising remain feasible.

What's the maximum number of passengers safe for a day-trip RIB from Šibenik?

A 9-meter RIB safely accommodates 8-10 passengers depending on weight distribution and sea state. Children occupy minimal displacement; families with 4-6 adults and 2-4 children fit comfortably with allocated life jacket provision. assigns vessel size based on passenger manifest to ensure safety margins.

Can motorboat skippers without formal certification charter from D-Marin?

Yes. Motorboat operation in Croatian waters (within 12 nm of coast) doesn't mandate formal certification for recreational use, though recommends basic RIB familiarization briefings. A 30-minute pre-departure orientation covers throttle response, trim adjustment, and emergency procedures. Boat4You's standard process ensures all skippers demonstrate competence before departure.

How does a motorboat navigate Kornati's shallow coves?

RIBs draw 0.6-0.8 meters, enabling approach to 2-3-meter anchorages that other vessels cannot safely access. Modern GPS chartplotters display bathymetric detail; combined with visual assessment of water color (dark blue = deep, light turquoise = shallow), experienced skippers anchor confidently in 2.5-meter depths.

What should we pack for a full-day Kornati motorboat trip?

Sun protection (high SPF lotion, hat, sunglasses), swimwear, towel, light lunch and snacks, plenty of fresh water (1.5-2 liters per person), underwater camera, and a light jacket for spray exposure. Motorboats provide minimal weather protection; RIB trips involve sustained sun exposure and salt spray.

Is it possible to combine motorboat day trips with overnight sailing?

Yes. Many charter clients charter a motorboat for fast day exploration (Kornati, Krka), then book overnight sailing or catamaran passages for slower-paced island-hopping. This hybrid approach maximizes itinerary diversity within a single charter week.
