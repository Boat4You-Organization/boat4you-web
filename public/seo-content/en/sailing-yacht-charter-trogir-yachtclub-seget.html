Sailing Seasons & Wind Patterns

Spring (April–May) offers the Maestral's most reliable thermal strength, with afternoon northwesterlies 8–14 knots ideal for learning and extended passages. Summer (June–August) brings consistent Maestral patterns, though July and August can intensify to 20+ knots—energizing for experienced crews, challenging for novices. Autumn (September–October) delivers warm water, moderate winds 10–16 knots, and the fewest crowds—many serious sailors prefer autumn for long-distance exploration.
