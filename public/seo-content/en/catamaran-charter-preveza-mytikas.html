Preveza: The Historic Sailing Hub

Preveza has long been a cornerstone of Ionian sailing, famous for its connection to the Battle of Actium. Today, it's a thriving charter base with modern amenities and rich historical significance. The town combines Greek hospitality with excellent provisioning opportunities, making it ideal for families and experienced sailors alike. Boat4You partners with local operators to ensure your catamaran is fully equipped and ready for the Ionian's summer Maistro northwest breeze, typically 10-18 knots.

Mytikas: The Emerging Coastal Village

Nestled on the western Greek mainland between Aktion and Palairos, Mytikas is a hidden gem facing the Kalamos and Kastos islands. This traditional coastal village features a small harbour with modern pontoon berths—perfect for sailors seeking quieter anchorages than the busier Lefkada hotspots. Mytikas appeals to adventurous charterers looking to explore less-traveled waters while maintaining easy access to Ionian highlights.

Perfect Sailing Season & Weather

The Ionian Islands enjoy one of Greece's most favorable sailing climates. From May through September, expect consistent Maistro winds ranging from 10-18 knots, ideal for catamaran handling and safe anchoraging. The combination of protected bays, dramatic coastal landscapes, and numerous islands creates varied day-sailing options. Summer conditions ensure warm water temperatures and excellent visibility for underwater activities and swimming.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to charter a catamaran in Preveza?

May through September offers optimal conditions, with consistent Maistro winds and warm temperatures. June and early September are ideal for avoiding peak August crowds while maintaining excellent weather and sea conditions.

Can I sail from Mytikas to major Ionian destinations?

Yes, Mytikas provides convenient access to Lefkada, Syvota, Kalamos, and Kastos islands. Day-sails typically range 15-30 nautical miles, perfect for mixed-ability sailing groups and families.

Does Preveza Marina offer winter storage?

Cleopatra Marina in Preveza is the largest winter boatyard in the Ionian with 600+ yacht storage spaces and technical refit facilities, making it ideal for extended boat storage and maintenance projects.

What sailing experience is required for Ionian catamaran charters?

Both bareboat and crewed options are available. Bareboat charterers should hold an RYA Day Skipper certificate or equivalent experience. Beginners and non-sailing groups can book fully crewed charters or take professional instruction courses beforehand.
