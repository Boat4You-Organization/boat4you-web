Adriatic Sailing: Seasons, Winds & Strategy

Spring Maestral (April–May) delivers reliable 10–16 knot afternoons, ideal for training cruises and long-distance passages. Summer (June–August) brings lighter winds, shorter sailing days, and crowded anchorages. Autumn (September–October) repeats spring: strong Maestral, excellent upwind conditions, and manageable crew fatigue. Bura episodes (sudden northeasterlies exceeding 20+ knots) require shelter or careful passage planning but offer thrilling sailing for experienced crews.

Sailing strategists discuss wind patterns, current flows, and optimal departure timing. Local MCI members share observed conditions and seasonal patterns.
