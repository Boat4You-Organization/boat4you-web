Luxury Day Charters and Sunset Cruises

A full-day motor yacht charter from Ornos might start with a leisurely breakfast aboard, followed by transit to Delos for ancient temple exploration and Rhenia's pristine beaches. Afternoon swims, gourmet lunch served on the flybridge, and selective shopping at hidden coves round out the experience. Most day charters include a professional skipper and stewardess to handle navigation, provisioning, and hospitality.

Sunset cruises are equally memorable: depart Ornos in late afternoon, navigate south to Mykonos' dramatic southwestern cliffs, enjoy an aperitif on deck while the sun dips toward Crete, and return to Ornos Beach under starlight. These experiences are best enjoyed aboard a motor yacht with climate control, elegant styling, and attentive crew.

Delos Archaeological Excursions and Cultural Experiences

The island of Delos, birthplace of Apollo according to Greek mythology, lies 30 km east of Ornos. A motor yacht expedition allows flexibility unavailable to land-based tourists: arrive early to beat tour groups, hire a private guide, and explore ancient temples, the sacred lake, and the House of the Dolphins at a leisurely pace. FAQ

Do I need experience to charter a motor yacht?

No. Motor yachts in our Ornos fleet come with professional skippers and crew who handle all navigation and operations. Your role is purely to enjoy the experience. We require no prior boating knowledge.

Are meals and beverages included in the charter?

Standard charters include basic provisioning and crew services. Gourmet meals and premium beverages are available for additional fees. We accommodate dietary restrictions and can arrange special menus with advance notice.

What is the weather like in Ornos during peak season?

May through September offers warm, sunny days (25–30°C) with gentle to moderate northerly winds. July–August can see afternoon winds (Force 4–5), creating excellent sailing but requiring experienced navigation. June and September are milder and ideal for luxury cruising.

Can we anchor overnight in Ornos Bay?

Yes. Ornos Bay has a designated mooring field and excellent holding in sandy bottom. Night anchoring is perfectly safe, and the bay's calm, protected waters make for restful sleep. Alternatively, nearby marinas offer berths if you prefer full facilities.

How far in advance should I book a motor yacht?

Peak season (July–August) should be booked 2–3 months in advance. Shoulder season (May–June, September) allows 4–6 weeks. Last-minute bookings (1–2 weeks ahead) are possible outside peak season, subject to availability. maintains a live availability calendar for real-time checking.

What happens if weather forces us to cancel or reschedule?

Safety is paramount. If severe weather (gale-force winds, rough seas) makes cruising unsafe, we reschedule your charter free of charge or offer a full refund. monitors forecasts and will contact you 48 hours before departure if conditions deteriorate. Minor wind or rain does not cancel charters—motor yachts are well-equipped to handle normal Aegean weather.
