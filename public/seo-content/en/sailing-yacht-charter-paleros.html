Navigation & Safety Systems

Modern sailing yachts include GPS chart plotters, depth sounders, wind instruments, and VHF radios. Many feature autopilots reducing crew fatigue during longer passages. Safety equipment includes life jackets, harnesses, liferafts, first aid kits, and emergency communication devices.

The Ionian's well-charted waters, coastal proximity, and active maritime community create a supportive sailing environment. Regular weather checks, conservative passage planning, and proper seamanship techniques minimize risks while maximizing enjoyment.
