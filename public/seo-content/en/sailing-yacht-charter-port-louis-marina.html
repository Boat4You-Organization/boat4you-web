Performance Sailing in Caribbean Trade Winds

The northeast trade winds that characterize Caribbean sailing from December through May provide ideal conditions for performance-focused sailing yacht operations. These consistent 15-25 knot breezes allow impressive sailing speeds while maintaining manageable weather conditions. Our sailing yachts respond eagerly to these winds, delivering the thrilling sailing experiences that sailors seek.

Port Louis Marina's location provides direct access to passage routes throughout the Grenadines and Caribbean.
