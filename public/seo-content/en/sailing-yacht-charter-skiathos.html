How challenging is Mediterranean sailing from Skiathos for intermediate sailors?

Sporades summer conditions—consistent Meltemi winds, clear visibility, sheltered island geography—suit intermediate sailors well. Daily sailing distances (2-5 hours) prevent fatigue. Can monohull sailing yachts anchor safely at Skiathos island beaches?

Professional crews select appropriately sized anchorages, deploy adequate anchor scope, and monitor holding throughout anchorage periods. Summer conditions provide exceptional stability and safety.
