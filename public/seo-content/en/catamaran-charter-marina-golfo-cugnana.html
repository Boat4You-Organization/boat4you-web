La Maddalena Archipelago and Nearby Excursions

La Maddalena archipelago, positioned 25 nautical miles northwest of Marina Golfo Cugnana, remains accessible within comfortable morning-departure cruising windows. Boat4You crews possess detailed knowledge of safe approaches to each anchorage, permitting confident navigation through the archipelago's complex channel system. Tavolara Marine Protected Area Discovery

The Tavolara Marine Protected Area, situated 15 nautical miles east-northeast of Marina Golfo Cugnana, protects dramatic Tavolara Island with its distinctive 565-meter limestone cliffs and companion Isola Molara. This UNESCO-designated protected area maintains pristine marine ecosystems supporting endangered Mediterranean seabirds and diverse fish populations. Catamaran excursions throughout the protected zone enable intimate wildlife observation, underwater exploration, and sunrise/sunset photography capturing dramatic cliff-side landscapes.

Protected status prohibits commercial fishing and limits vessel activity, ensuring crystal-clear water conditions with visibility frequently exceeding 25 meters. Snorkeling expeditions reveal dense seagrass meadows, rocky outcroppings harboring octopuses and spiny lobsters, and occasional Mediterranean monk seal encounters. Guided dives through our partner dive operators unveil submerged cave systems and archaeological discoveries within this carefully preserved marine sanctuary. Water sports equipment—snorkeling gear, paddleboards, and kayaks—enable diverse recreational pursuits throughout your Marina Golfo Cugnana-based charter.
