How do Mediterranean mooring systems work at Agia Effimia?

Med-mooring positions vessels perpendicular to the quay. You set a stern anchor in the seabed, then motor forward to tie bow lines to quay bollards. This arrangement allows harbor packing while keeping vessels accessible.
