Marina di Stabia: Southern Europe's Premier Boating Destination

Marina di Stabia represents a transformative investment in Mediterranean maritime infrastructure. Originally founded to serve the region's fishing industry, the facility evolved into a full-service luxury marina catering to superyachts, racing vessels, and private charters. Today, its 900-plus berths accommodate everything from modest sailboats to 120-meter expedition yachts, all within a protected basin and secure perimeter.

The facility's architectural design complements the region's maritime heritage while incorporating cutting-edge technology. Modern crane systems handle vessel hauling and storage, fuel delivery vessels provide efficient bunkering, and comprehensive waste management systems exceed environmental regulations. Marina di Stabia's commitment to sustainability and operational excellence has earned recognition from international maritime organizations.

Gateway to the Mediterranean

Marina di Stabia's strategic location positions your catamaran charter within easy reach of legendary destinations. Sorrento lies 18 kilometers north, accessible in roughly two hours by catamaran. Capri Island sits 20 nautical miles southwest, making overnight anchorages feasible for island exploration. Positano and the Amalfi Coast lie 25 nautical miles west along one of Europe's most dramatic coastlines.

Northward routes reach the Phlegrean Islands, volcanic formations rising dramatically from the sea, and the island of Ischia, known for thermal springs and hilltop monasteries. Southward exploration reveals the Cilento Coast's remote fishing villages, crystal-clear coves, and underwater archaeological sites. Yourcrew selects itineraries matching your interests, fitness, and desired pace of exploration.

Frequently Asked Questions
