Motor Yacht Charter at Flisvos Marina

Boat4You offers premium motor yacht charters based at Flisvos Marina, the Saronic Gulf's most prestigious yachting destination. Located in Palaio Faliro just 15 kilometers from central Athens and close to Syntagma Square, Flisvos is the premier base for luxury yacht charters combining five-star marina facilities with Mediterranean exploration.

About Flisvos Marina and Boat4You

Flisvos Marina is an exclusive mooring facility catering to superyachts and luxury vessels. With 303 dedicated berths, comprehensive provisioning services, and a team of concierge specialists, Flisvos is where Athens's elite yacht owners moor their vessels. Boat4You operates premium motor yachts from this world-class facility, giving you access to five-star amenities and professional support.

The marina's infrastructure includes 24-hour security, fuel dock with premium-grade diesel, freshwater hookups, power pedestals with 220V and 380V connections, and sewage pump-out facilities. WiFi covers the entire facility. Flisvos also hosts the Hellenic Maritime Museum and numerous upscale restaurants.

The marina's location near Syntagma Square (Athens's central hub) means you're minutes from the National Museum, Plaka district, and the city's best dining and entertainment.

Marina Facilities and Concierge Services

As a Boat4You charter guest mooring at Flisvos, you have access to full marina facilities including laundry, showers, and a modern reception area. The concierge team can arrange provisioning, marine services, restaurant reservations, and tickets to Athens attractions.

The Flisvos fuel dock dispenses premium marine diesel at competitive rates. Water is available at each berth. Waste disposal facilities meet international standards, and the marina maintains impeccable maintenance standards befitting its prestigious status. Flisvos offers direct access to expert marine engineers and specialists.

Premium Day Routes from Flisvos

From Flisvos Marina, set sail toward the Saronic Gulf's most beautiful islands. Aegina, the closest island, is just 1.5 hours away and offers the Temple of Aphaia, fresh pistachio farms, and authentic tavernas. Poros, a charming island nestled between hills, takes 2 hours and rewards visitors with secluded anchorages.

For a longer expedition, Hydra is a 3-hour sail from Flisvos. This traffic-free island is famous for its 18th-century architecture, donkey transportation, and artistic community. Overnight charters to Hydra are increasingly popular. Sunset cruises from Flisvos along the Athens Riviera coastline are spectacular, with views of the Acropolis illuminated at dusk.

Seasons and Optimal Visiting Periods

The Saronic Gulf's sailing season peaks from May to September, with July and August being warmest but busiest. Spring (May–June) and fall (September) offer ideal conditions with moderate temperatures, reliable winds, and fewer crowds.

Summer water temperatures reach 24–26°C, perfect for swimming and water sports. The Meltemia winds (northerly) blow steadily, providing consistent sailing conditions. recommends May–June or September–October for the best combination of weather, crowds, and value.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Flisvos Marina accessible by public transport?

Yes, Flisvos is on the metro line (Line 3, the Blue Line) connecting directly to Syntagma Square and Athens Airport. Buses also serve the area. Taxis and Uber are readily available.

Can we combine a city break with a yacht charter?

Absolutely! This is ideal at Flisvos. Spend days exploring Athens, then take afternoon/sunset cruises. Multi-day charters to Aegina, Poros, or Hydra can be seamlessly combined with city sightseeing.

What's the difference between Flisvos and Palaio Faliro charters?

Flisvos Marina offers premium facility access and concierge services. Palaio Faliro-based charters are slightly more casual and cost-effective. Both access the same islands and waters.

Are children welcome aboard?

Yes, families are welcome. Our yachts include spacious cabins suitable for families. We recommend calm-weather charters and nearby islands for children's first experiences.

Can we arrange special events like proposals aboard?

Yes, Flisvos is famous for romantic charters. We can arrange champagne, candlelit dinners, and sunset views for unforgettable celebrations. Professional photography documents your special moments.
