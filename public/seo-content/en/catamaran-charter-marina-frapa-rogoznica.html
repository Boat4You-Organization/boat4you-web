Marina Frapa: Your Catamaran Charter Base

Marina Frapa holds the coveted Blue Flag designation and consistently ranks among Croatia's finest marinas. The facility accommodates up to 462 wet berths and 150 dry berths, with pontoons rated for yachts up to 35 meters. For catamaran charters, this translates to secure moorings, professional on-site staff, and reliable turnaround logistics.

Full-Service Facilities for Catamaran Preparation

The marina operates a 50-ton travel lift, repair yard, and full chandlery. Fuel, water, and shore power are available 24/7. On-site amenities include a hotel, three restaurants, fitness facilities, and swimming pool—allowing crew to explore the marina grounds while your catamaran is provisioned and serviced.

Strategic Location Between Major Towns

Rogoznica sits approximately 25 minutes from Šibenik and 30 minutes from Trogir. Both towns offer cultural attractions, historic Old Towns, and renowned seafood dining. The proximity to these centers means your catamaran charter can include onshore excursions without extensive sailing time.

Sailing Routes from Marina Frapa Rogoznica

A catamaran's shallow draft and maneuverability open passages that traditional monohulls struggle to access. From Marina Frapa, you can navigate into protected bay systems, anchor near small islands, and explore hidden fishing villages that define authentic Adriatic cruising.

Kornati National Park: Hour-Long Sail

The 140-island archipelago of Kornati lies roughly one hour's sail from the marina. Catamarans glide between the islands' limestone cliffs, providing stable platforms for snorkeling and underwater photography. The national park offers three designated anchorages where you can spend entire days exploring without constantly repositioning.

Primošten and Local Wine Country

Just 10 minutes by road (or a short sail along the coast), Primošten's vineyards produce the renowned Babić red wine. Many catamaran charters combine nautical exploration with culinary stops—visit local wine bars, sample grilled fish prepared in the traditional peka style, and enjoy fresh shellfish at harborside taverns.

Protected Bays and Island Anchorages

The waters between Rogoznica and Trogir contain numerous sheltered bays. Catamarans can reach depths of less than 2 meters in many locations, allowing access to small islands and coves that require careful navigation or simply aren't reachable by deeper-draft vessels. These shallow areas yield the best fishing and snorkeling opportunities.

Seasonality and Weather Patterns

Marina Frapa operates year-round, though the optimal catamaran season spans late May through September. Summer winds typically blow from the northwest (Maestral), providing consistent sailing conditions in the morning with afternoon calms. The Scirocco (southeast wind) occasionally brings heavier swells but also exciting downwind passages.

Spring (April–May) and autumn (September–October) offer milder temperatures, fewer crowds, and challenging but rewarding sailing conditions. Winter conditions are less predictable, with occasional strong northeasterly winds and rain, though protected anchorages around Rogoznica provide adequate shelter.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a bareboat catamaran charter possible from Marina Frapa Rogoznica?

Yes, experienced sailors can arrange bareboat catamarans at Marina Frapa. The marina's professional staff and on-site support make it an ideal location for independent sailing. Boat4You offers bareboat and skipper-inclusive options to match your crew's experience level.

What's the difference between a catamaran and a monohull charter?

Catamarans provide wider decks, less heeling, faster speeds in light winds, and easier anchoring in shallow waters. Monohulls offer better upwind performance and typically lower rental costs. For family cruising or accessible comfort, catamarans are often preferred.

Can a catamaran reach the shallow waters around Zmajevo Oko?

Zmajevo Oko, the famous heart-shaped lake near Rogoznica, is accessible by road rather than water. However, a catamaran's shallow draft allows approach to nearby coastal inlets and small islands that require similar anchoring skills and local knowledge.

Are meals and shore excursions included in catamaran charters?

Base charter rates typically cover vessel rental and berthing. Provisioning, fuel, crew meals, and guided excursions are usually optional add-ons. Marina Frapa's on-site restaurants and proximity to Šibenik and Trogir make self-catering or restaurant dining straightforward.
