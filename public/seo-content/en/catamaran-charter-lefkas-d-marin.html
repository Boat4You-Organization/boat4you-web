Catamaran Sailing from Lefkas: The Ionian Loop

From D-Marin Lefkas, a catamaran opens the entire central Ionian to you. The Maistro northwesterly breeze averages 10–18 knots in summer, creating reliable, flat-water sailing. Head south toward Meganisi (6nm), a low-key island with idyllic anchorages at Vathy and Spilia. Continue to Scorpios (10nm), then Kalamos and Kastos (emerald islets, minimal crowds). Or venture east to Ithaca (the mythological home of Odysseus) and nearby Kefalonia, where Fiskardo's harborside tavernas anchor the northeast corner of your cruising ground.
