Frequently Asked Questions

What separates motor yachts from other charter vessel types?

Motor yachts prioritize engineered comfort, sophisticated design, and refined hospitality over sailing experience. Professional crews handle all navigation; luxury interiors rival upscale hotels. Motor yachts suit guests valuing privacy, convenience, and consistent comfort above active sailing engagement.

How many guests do motor yachts accommodate?

Typical motor yachts accommodate 4–12 guests across private staterooms. Larger vessels accommodate 16+ guests for group charters and corporate events. Crew cabins remain separate, maintaining guest privacy and crew professionalism.

Can motor yachts anchor near La Maddalena's pristine beaches?

Yes. Modern motor yachts feature shallow-draft designs enabling secure anchoring in 2–5 meters adjacent to Budelli, Caprera, and Porto Madonna anchorages. Professional skippers optimize anchor selection and holding power ensuring safety in moderate Mistral wind conditions.

What happens during rough weather?

Motor yachts seek protected anchorages or return to Cannigione. La Maddalena's harbors provide exceptional weather protection. maintains flexible rescheduling and comprehensive insurance covering weather-related disruptions. Professional crews prioritize guest safety above schedule adherence.
