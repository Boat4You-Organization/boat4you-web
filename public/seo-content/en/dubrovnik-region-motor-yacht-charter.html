Seasons and Southern Adriatic Conditions

Spring (April–May): Cool air, moderate winds, unpredictable weather. Motor yachts' speed and professional crew handle conditions confidently; luxury amenities provide refuge during brief squalls.

Summer (June–August): Ideal. Warm days (26–32°C), calm seas, long daylight, vibrant provisioning and cultural activity. July–August crowd Dubrovnik waterfront; May–June and September offer less congestion.

Autumn (September–October): Warm water (24–25°C), moderate variable winds, thinning crowds. Connoisseur's season: golden light, comfortable sailing, excellent provisioning, reduced resort congestion.

Winter (November–March): Limited operations; only hardy crews and shortest itineraries attempted.
