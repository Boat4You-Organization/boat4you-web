Why Choose a Catamaran from Marina Kremik?

Catamarans excel in Croatian waters due to their shallow draft and inherent stability. Marina Kremik's protected natural bay provides secure mooring for these twin-hulled vessels, and the marina's ~380 berths accommodate various sizes. The shallow-draft advantage means greater access to coves and anchorages around Kornati National Park and the nearby Šibenik Channel.

Catamaran rentals from Boat4You's Marina Kremik selection offer spacious cabins, excellent beam-to-length ratios, and the comfort preferred by families and groups. The platform stability reduces motion, making longer passages more comfortable for those sensitive to wave conditions.

Space and Comfort on the Water

Unlike monohulls, catamarans distribute living space across two hulls, creating separate cabins and generous saloon areas. Charter catamarans from Marina Kremik typically offer 4–8 berths with well-appointed galleys and heads. The spacious cockpit provides protection and room for entertaining, essential when island-hopping or anchoring in Kornati's limestone bays.

Marina Kremik: Facilities and Location

Marina Kremik serves as a full-service departure point with fuel, water, electricity, and professional repair facilities available year-round. The marina's crane system supports haul-outs and maintenance work. Located 17 km south of Šibenik and 7 km north of Rogoznica, the marina sits within easy sailing range of major Dalmatian destinations.

Primošten itself is a working fishing village with excellent provisioning options. Local restaurants serve fresh grilled fish and Dalmatian specialties. The town's terraced vineyards produce Babić wine, a distinctive local red that pairs well with Mediterranean fare. Markets and shops cater to provisioning needs before multi-day passages.

Gateway to Protected Anchorages

Marina Kremik's position provides quick access to Kornati National Park, approximately 1.5 hours sail northeastward. The 140-island archipelago offers protected anchorages, white-stone beaches, and pristine waters ideal for catamaran exploration. The Šibenik Channel to the north features medieval town cultures and historical depth.

Seasonal Considerations for Catamaran Charter

Marina Kremik remains accessible year-round, though catamaran charter availability peaks April through October. Spring months (April–May) offer light winds and warm days, ideal for families and leisurely exploration. Summer (June–August) brings stronger Maestral winds, perfect for keen sailors but busier anchorages in Kornati.

Autumn (September–October) delivers stable weather, fewer crowds, and comfortable sailing conditions. Winter charters are possible but less common due to reduced daylight and occasional strong southeasterly winds.
