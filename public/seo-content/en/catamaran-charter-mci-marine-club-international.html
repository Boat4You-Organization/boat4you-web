MCI Fleet: Size, Specifications & Comfort

MCI's catamaran portfolio ranges from 38 to 48 feet. A 38-foot vessel accommodates 6–8 passengers across three spacious cabins; 48-foot catamarans host up to 10 with four cabins and crew quarters. Modern hulls integrate air-conditioning, integrated galley systems, and en-suite heads (one per cabin). Saloons feature dining areas, entertainment systems, and navigation stations accessible to bareboat captains.

Deck layouts emphasize entertainment: large forward trampolines suited to sunbathing, protected helm stations, and aft cockpit bars. Swimming platforms at stern simplify water access. Many MCI catamarans feature solar panels, saltwater-shower systems, and wind-generation capability—technical sophistication matching luxury-yacht standards while maintaining catamaran stability and efficiency.

FAQ: Catamaran Charters from MCI Marine Club International

Can we book a bareboat catamaran at MCI without sailing experience?

MCI encourages professional skipper arrangements for non-experienced groups. Bareboat charters require International Certificates of Competence; Boat4You's vetting process assesses credentials. If bareboat isn't suitable, professional skippers (€250–€400 daily) provide expertise alongside learning opportunities for aspiring navigators.

What's included in the concierge provisioning service?

Breakfast staples, lunch ingredients, and dinner proteins/vegetables are pre-provisioned and stored onboard before you arrive. Beverages (alcoholic and non-alcoholic), specialty items, and restaurant reservations are coordinated through MCI offices. Costs typically range 30–50 EUR per person daily for provisioning; dining reservations are separate. Vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-sensitive menus are handled seamlessly.

How is MCI different from West Harbour or East Harbour charters?

West and East Harbour offer working-port authenticity and competitive pricing. MCI provides boutique intimacy, concierge-level service, and curated guest experiences. If you value operational efficiency, city-center access, and casual atmosphere, West Harbour suits you. If you prefer exclusive, service-oriented environments with personalized attention, MCI delivers. Catamaran quality across all three bases is comparable; differentiation is operational philosophy.

Can we include family activities like snorkeling lessons or diving?

Absolutely. MCI's concierge network includes certified diving instructors, snorkeling guides, and activity coordinators. These services are arranged pre-charter and seamlessly integrated into daily schedules. Children's activities, beginner-friendly diving certifications, and customized excursions are MCI specialties.

Is MCI suitable for corporate incentive charters?

Yes. MCI's boutique scale accommodates team-building experiences, executive retreats, and milestone celebrations. coordinates custom arrangements: multi-boat charter formations, professional facilitation, provisioning coordination, and activity planning. Dozens of corporate clients charter from MCI annually; the club's membership community enhances networking opportunities.
