Can I add a skipper or hostess after booking the bareboat?

Yes. arranges professional skippers and hostesses on short notice, subject to availability. Rates average €180–€250 per day for a skipper, €150–€200 for a hostess, plus food-kitty contribution.

What happens if weather cancels a planned leg?

Your skipper recommends shelter or an alternative route. Flexibility is part of the experience; we do not force risky passages. support is a phone call away for reroute advice.
