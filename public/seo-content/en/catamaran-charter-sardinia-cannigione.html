Weather and Sailing Conditions in Cannigione

Sardinia's northeast coast enjoys a Mediterranean climate with warm, dry summers (June–September) and mild winters. The prevailing Maestrale wind, blowing from the northwest, typically arrives in the afternoon, building to 12-18 knots. Catamarans handle these conditions beautifully, offering neutral helm balance and minimal weatherhelming even in 20-knot gusts.

Spring (April–May) and autumn (September–October) present optimal sailing windows: steady breezes, comfortable water temperatures (18-24°C), and fewer day-trippers crowding the anchorages. Winter months see occasional mistral storms and variable winds, but Cannigione's protected basin and robust mooring systems ensure safe harbor.

Our team monitors Meteo storms, swell forecasts, and wind patterns, empowering you to make informed decisions about timing and destinations. Mobile apps synced to the vessel's navigation system provide 10-day forecasts and satellite imagery.
