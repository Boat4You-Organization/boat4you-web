How much fuel does a motor yacht consume daily?

Consumption depends on speed and engine configuration. Cruising at 8–10 knots, expect 8–15 gallons per hour. At 12+ knots, consumption rises to 15–25 gallons hourly. provides fuel-cost estimates based on your planned itinerary.

What's the maximum range on a full fuel tank?

Typical motor yachts cruise 250–350 nautical miles at economical speeds. Fuel stops at Hvar, Vis, or Korčula extend range infinitely. Your captain plans fuel logistics to avoid running low.

Are motor yachts air-conditioned?

All West Coast motor yachts feature AC in cabins and salon. Engine-generated power or generator systems maintain cool comfort even during stationary daytime anchoring.

What certifications do bareboat captains require?

An International Certificate of Competence (ICC) or equivalent national credential satisfies requirements. Boat4You's process verifies credentials and recommends hired-crew alternatives for less-experienced skippers.
