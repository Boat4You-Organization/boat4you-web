Motor Yacht Routes: Kornati, Telašćica & Executive Anchorages

Kornati National Park (5 nautical miles, 30 minutes) offers dramatic limestone islands and protected anchorages. Motor yachts moor in exclusive spots like Sipan's sheltered bay, Katić's fish-restaurant cove, and Žut's pristine anchorage. The shallow Adriatic waters mean secure holding and accessible beach clubs.

Telašćica Bay (18 nautical miles, 60 minutes) provides one of the Mediterranean's most prestigious anchorages. Sheer 150-meter cliffs, a natural saltwater lake, and protected deep-water mooring create an unforgettable backdrop for entertainment or contemplative retreats. Motor yachts anchor in security while crews explore the bay by tender.

Pašman and Ugljan islands (8–12 nautical miles, 25–35 minutes) offer upscale village anchorages, provisioning stops, and exclusive waterfront restaurants welcoming motor yacht clientele. Multi-day charters often anchor between these islands, exploring both shores and enjoying village hospitality.

Dugi Otok's southern coast (15–20 nautical miles, 45–75 minutes) features pristine Sakarun beach and remote anchorages—destinations accessible exclusively by water. Motor yachts anchor while crews enjoy beach tenders and water sports.
