Frequently Asked Questions

How do Mediterranean moorings (Med-mooring) work?

Med-mooring positions vessels perpendicular to the quay. You set a stern anchor in the seabed 10-15 meters offshore, then motor forward to tie bow lines to quay bollards. This arrangement allows dense harbor packing while keeping vessels accessible.
