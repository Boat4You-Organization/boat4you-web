Best Sailing Seasons

Spring (April–May) brings warm air, calm mornings ideal for shakedown passages, and manageable crowds. Water temperatures reach 16–18°C. Summer (June–August) delivers reliable wind and social anchorages—though mid-July through mid-August intensifies crowding and heat. Autumn (September–October) offers the region's finest sailing: warm water (21–23°C), consistent wind, and golden light perfect for photographs and cultural exploration. Winter allows passage sailing but brings shorter days and occasional gales.
